# AGENTS.md — Le Pacha 2 (Landing Page)

## 🎯 Objective

Build a **modern, mobile-first landing page** for a Turkish fast-food / kebab restaurant.

Main goals:

👉 Drive conversions (Uber Eats / phone orders)  
👉 Make users hungry instantly  
👉 Create trust (clean, generous, fast service)

---

## 🧠 Positioning

Le Pacha 2 is:

- Fast
- Generous
- Affordable
- Casual
- Halal
- Comfort food

⚠️ BUT the website must feel **more premium than a typical kebab shop**

---

## 🎨 Art Direction (VERY IMPORTANT)

### ❌ Avoid (already used in other projects like Nefis)
- White / clean minimalist layouts
- Fine dining / luxury restaurant style
- Bright / soft colors

### ✅ Required style (DIFFERENT)

**Mood:**
- Dark / night
- Street food modern
- Grill & fire atmosphere

**Colors:**
- Charcoal black (#0D0D0D)
- Dark grey (#1A1A1A)
- Grill orange (#FF6A00)
- Off-white (#F5F5F5)

**Visual inspiration:**
- Fire 🔥
- Grilled meat
- Neon / night vibe

**UI effects:**
- Subtle orange glow
- Deep shadows
- Elevated cards
- Strong contrast

---

## 🧱 Page Structure

### 1. HERO (CRITICAL)

Content:
- Title: **Le Pacha 2**
- Tagline:
  > "The taste of the grill, fast and generous."

CTA:
- Primary: 👉 "Order on Uber Eats"
- Secondary: 👉 "Call now"

Visual:
- Large kebab / grilled meat / burger
- Dark background with warm glow

---

### 2. SOCIAL PROOF

- ⭐ Rating (~4.2 based on real data)
- Short customer reviews (realistic tone)

Examples:
- "Fast and delicious"
- "Very generous portions"
- "Always consistent"

---

### 3. MENU HIGHLIGHTS

Show categories only:

- Kebabs / Sandwiches
- Plates (mixed grill, chicken, merguez)
- Tacos / Tex-Mex
- Burgers

⚠️ Do NOT show full menu → just trigger desire

---

### 4. WHY CHOOSE US

3–4 blocks:

- 🔥 Flame-grilled meat
- ⚡ Fast service
- 🥙 Generous portions
- 🌙 Perfect for late meals

---

### 5. PRACTICAL INFO

- 📍 Address: 154 Boulevard Henri Barbusse, 91210 Draveil
- 📞 Phone: +33 1 69 42 69 34
- 🕒 Hours: ~11:00 – 22:00

---

### 6. FINAL CTA

Strong conversion block:

👉 "Hungry? Order now."

Buttons:
- Uber Eats
- Call

---

## 📱 Mobile UX (MANDATORY)

- Mobile-first design
- Large tap targets
- Sticky bottom bar:
  - "Order"
  - "Call"

---

## ⚡ Conversion Rules

- CTA always visible
- No friction
- Fast loading
- Clear hierarchy

---

## 🔎 Local SEO

Include keywords:

- kebab Draveil
- halal restaurant Draveil
- tacos Draveil
- fast food Draveil

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- Next.js
- Tailwind CSS
- Optimized images
- No backend required

---

## 🚫 DO NOT

- Use generic templates
- Add too much text
- Make it look like fine dining
- Add useless gallery

---

## 💡 Bonus (optional)

- WhatsApp button
- Hover animations on food
- Subtle heat / smoke effect

---

## 🎯 Expected Result

A website that:

- Makes users hungry in 3 seconds
- Feels modern and clean
- Drives immediate action (order / call)
